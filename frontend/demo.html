Our Facilities

Everything you need under one roof — from the gym floor to the pool to the sauna.

🏋️
Gym Floor
60+ cardio machines, free weights up to 50kg, resistance machines, functional training rig, and a dedicated stretching zone.
🏊
Swimming Pool
25-metre, 6-lane pool with daily lane swimming, aqua aerobics, and family swim sessions. Lifeguards on duty at all times.
🧖
Sauna & Steam
Dedicated sauna and steam room for recovery. Included in Premium membership, available as a supplement for Standard members.
🚴
Spin Studio
Dedicated cycling studio with 18 bikes. Home to our popular Spin, Morning Cycle, and Spin Express classes.
🥗
FuelZone Café
On-site café serving protein shakes, smoothies, healthy snacks, and hot drinks. Members receive up to 15% discount.
🚿
Changing Rooms
Male, female, and gender-neutral changing rooms with lockers, hot showers, hair dryers, and vanity areas.
