PEAKFORM GYM & LEISURE CENTRE — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
============================================================

GENERAL

Q: Where is PeakForm Gym located?
A: We are located at 14 Meridian Way, Loughborough, LE11 3GX. We have free parking available on-site with 120 spaces.

Q: What are your opening hours?
A: Monday–Friday: 06:00–22:00 | Saturday: 07:00–20:00 | Sunday: 08:00–18:00
Bank holidays may have reduced hours — check our website or call reception for updates.

Q: How do I contact the gym?
A: Phone: 01509 334 200 | Email: hello@peakformgym.co.uk | Reception is staffed during all opening hours.

Q: Is there parking available?
A: Yes, free on-site parking is available for all members and visitors. Disabled bays are located directly outside the main entrance.

Q: Do you have changing facilities?
A: Yes. We have male, female, and gender-neutral changing rooms, all equipped with lockers, showers, and hair dryers. Towels are available to hire at reception for £1.50.

---

MEMBERSHIP

Q: How do I join PeakForm?
A: You can join online at www.peakformgym.co.uk, via our app, or in person at reception. A valid photo ID is required for in-person sign-up.

Q: Is there a joining fee?
A: There is a one-off £20 joining fee for all new members. This is occasionally waived during promotional periods.

Q: Can I freeze my membership?
A: Yes. Members on monthly contracts can freeze their membership for up to 3 months per year for a small admin fee of £5/month. Annual members can freeze for up to 2 months at no charge.

Q: Can I cancel my membership?
A: Monthly rolling members can cancel with 30 days' written notice by emailing cancellations@peakformgym.co.uk. Annual members who cancel early may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Q: Can I use the gym if I'm not a member?
A: Yes! Day passes are available for £10 (off-peak) or £12 (peak, weekdays 07:00–09:30 and 17:00–19:30). These include full gym floor access.

Q: Do you offer guest passes?
A: Members can bring a guest for £6 per visit (maximum 3 visits per calendar month per guest). Guests must sign in at reception.

Q: Is there a minimum age to join?
A: Members must be 16 or over. Under-18s require written parental consent. Juniors aged 12–15 may attend supervised junior sessions only.

---

FITNESS CLASSES

Q: Are classes included in membership?
A: Yes — all group fitness classes are included in Standard and Premium memberships at no extra cost. Basic members pay £3 per class.

Q: How do I book a class?
A: Classes can be booked up to 7 days in advance via the PeakForm app, our website, or at reception. We recommend booking early as popular classes fill quickly.

Q: What happens if I miss a booked class?
A: If you cannot attend, please cancel at least 2 hours before the class starts to free up the space. Members who accumulate 3 or more no-shows in a calendar month may lose booking privileges for 14 days.

Q: Do I need to bring anything to classes?
A: Wear comfortable workout clothing and bring a water bottle. A small towel is recommended. Specialist equipment (mats, weights, etc.) is provided.

Q: What classes do you offer?
A: We offer over 40 classes per week including Spin, HIIT, Yoga, Pilates, BodyPump, Zumba, Boxing Fitness, Aqua Aerobics, and more. See the class schedule for full details.

---

FACILITIES

Q: What equipment is available on the gym floor?
A: We have 60+ cardio machines (treadmills, rowing machines, bikes, cross-trainers), a free weights area, resistance machines, a functional training rig, and a stretching/mobility zone.

Q: Do you have a swimming pool?
A: Yes. We have a 25-metre, 6-lane pool open to members during designated swim sessions. Lane swimming, aqua classes, and family swim sessions are available.

Q: Do you have a sauna or steam room?
A: Yes — both a sauna and steam room are available to Premium members. Standard and Basic members can access them for a £3 supplement per visit.

Q: Is there a café or nutrition bar?
A: Yes. The FuelZone café is open daily and serves protein shakes, smoothies, healthy snacks, and hot drinks. Members receive a 10% discount.

Q: Do you offer personal training?
A: Yes. We have a team of qualified personal trainers available for 1-to-1 sessions. Sessions start from £35/hour. Contact reception or speak to a trainer on the gym floor to book.

---

HEALTH & SAFETY

Q: Do I need a health assessment before using the gym?
A: New members are offered a complimentary fitness induction with a qualified trainer. This is optional but strongly recommended, particularly for those new to exercise or returning after injury.

Q: What if I have a medical condition?
A: Please inform reception of any medical conditions or injuries at sign-up. We may ask you to provide GP clearance before beginning certain activities.

Q: What is your emergency procedure?
A: Trained first-aiders are on duty at all times. Defibrillators are located at reception and poolside. In the event of an emergency, alert a member of staff immediately or call 999.
