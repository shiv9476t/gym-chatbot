MEMBERSHIP PLANS
----------------

BASIC MEMBERSHIP — £19.99/month

STANDARD MEMBERSHIP — £34.99/month

PREMIUM MEMBERSHIP — £49.99/month

  - Premium Annual: £499.99/year (saving ~£100)

STUDENT MEMBERSHIP — £24.99/month
  - Full gym floor access (all hours)
  - Unlimited group fitness classes (included)
  - Swimming pool access
  - Valid NUS/student ID required at sign-up and renewal

CORPORATE MEMBERSHIP — from £28.99/month per employee
  - Standard-tier benefits

  - Monthly invoicing available
  - Contact corporate@peakformgym.co.uk for a quote

FAMILY ADD-ON — £9.99/month per additional household member
  - Available for Standard and Premium members
  - Each additional member must be resident at same address
